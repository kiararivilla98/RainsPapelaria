About Us


My name is Raelene Jhuztyn Laxa, but I prefer to be called Rain. I am a second-year Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering student at Adamson University. I also serve as the Vice President of the Academic Scholars Organization, where I help guide and support fellow scholars in their academic journey.


Creativity has always been a part of me. My love for anime inspires my designs and fuels my passion for business. I believe that dreams become reality through action. Instead of waiting for opportunities, I choose to create them. No matter how small the step, moving forward is always better than standing still.




Why I Started My Business?




Business was never part of my plans, and I never thought it would be. I always believed that running a business required immense effort and patience, far more than simply studying a pile of subjects in school. There are countless factors to consider before starting, and for a long time, I never saw myself taking that path.


But as social media trends grew, I found myself drawn to paper crafting, something I had loved since childhood. Back in elementary school, I even sold origami in class, though it was just a hobby. I never imagined it could turn into something more.


Rain’s Papelaria started in December 2024, and thanks to our amazing customers, we’ve seen a rise in orders this January. The love and support have been overwhelming, pushing us to keep creating products that bring joy and creativity to everyday life.
